RESIDENTIAL INTERIOR DESIGN


Our aim with every interior design is a creation a space which reflecting your tastes and desires.
Rigorous attention to detail, we consider every detail of the style you live when planning your space.
We begin with define plan-view of your space and develop using realistic 3D photo imagery, and concludes with detailed plans.
Throughout the process, we work closely with external consultants, lighting and curtain designers, furniture manufacturers, which provide some masterpieces for you.

OFFICE INTERIOR DESIGN

PrimStudio is a leading global design practice specialising in the development of strategies and design solutions for commercial workplaces across a wide variety of industries.
Transforming workspaces is our mission statement – we know it takes a pragmatic, visionary, flexible and sustainable approach to maximize the potential of every workspace.
Unique and exciting work environment with interior design from PrimStudio, for clients and tenants alike, unfailingly achieves measurable results, meets individual business objectives and enhanced workplace performance and productivity...

COMMERCIAL INTERIOR DESIGN


We provide a range of interior design services for hotels, resorts, restaurants, and lounges.
Offering an individual and refreshing approach to interior design where creativity and vision meet the demands of the commercial environment. We recognise that the best results are achieved through a synthesis of concept, beauty and functional integration.
Slow food, fast food, upscale or informal, dining out—or just cocktails out—should be a memorable experience. Dynamic and detailed, our designs for these spaces anticipate a wonderful time.
