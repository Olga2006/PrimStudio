WELCOME TO PRIMSTUDIO





You are looking for designer in France in Provence-Alpes-Cote d'Azur in the department Alpes-Maritimes?
You've come to the right place!
We are Based in Mandelieu la Napoule, in the town of elegance and calm.
We glad to create spaces for people that reflect their needs and allow them to feel better and give value to their property.





OUR PROJECTS

INTERIOR DESIGN


PrimStudio delivers creative and functional interior design solutions based on impeccable selection the materials of finishes, textures, furniture, light and finish details corresponding of volume, natural light and shadow.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT


Our designers manage the whole project from conception to completion through construction phases.
All aspects of a scheme are considered and supervised, from flooring and window treatments to furniture and accessories

PROCUREMENT MANAGEMENT


We submit orders for equipment of finishes and furnishings, offering a vast array of exclusive furniture, fabrics, lighting and accessories of Italian and French top brands as well as exceptional handwork manufacturing, making your space really extraordinary.
Even if you need a luxury Italian furniture outside of France, we are ready to assist in the choice of the manufacturer, to order directly from the factory, and to organize of transportation right to your object with the best terms.





HOME STAGING


Increase the value of real estate performing a presale renovation!
If you want to make a property more profitable we will help you to meet the needs and wants of consumers as closely as possible acceleration of implementation the selling.

3D VISUALIZATION


Creation of photorealistic 3D images. The possibilities offered by this technology are endless and essential for architects and designers for correct understanding of a project by your client.
We can help you at any stage of the project, from modeling of unique furniture to design of final image of space.

FURNITURE DESIGN


Based on your vision, we design your future furniture which is very refined and remarkable.
Our connections with skilled craftsmen and producers of furniture in Italy and France allow you to express your individual taste.
