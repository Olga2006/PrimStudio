Project steps


PrimStudio offers drafting and space planning services which consist of 8 steps




1.Send the information about your project


Is it an entire building or several rooms? Sizes and kind of the rooms. Are you starting from scratch or re-decorating? Do you consider an alterations or extensions?
And after We will contact you shortly to coordinate time and place for the next step.

2.Introductory meeting


In this step a clear direction and scope for your project will be identified. We will get meeting to get to know you and your needs, preferences and your vision. We will discuss budget and timelines.

3.Visit of the object


In this step, the designer visits your object to gather more detailed information. We take measurements of your space noting electrical outlets, bulkheads, hearths, mantles, door swings, and so on.

4.Plan View


The designer creates a scaled plan-view of your space. We focus on the practical considerations like size, proportion, and function. We determine the optimal objects and where it is situated in the room. After we meet with you to review the plans and finish selections and make any refinements needed.

5.Design Presentation


We select your new interior finishes. We develop a cohesive palette of colours, materials, textures, build and finish details. This is the phase when any designed pieces or treatments would be sketched for your review.

6.Construction Documentation


We detail out all of your drawings so your contractor knows exactly what our design intent is and has accurate drawings to price out his job.

7.Delivery & Setup


We also draft and submit purchase orders for all of your equipment (interior finishes, furnishings,...) to ensure delivery to site and installation.

8.Construction


We visit the job site frequently to oversee the subcontractors and ensure work is progressing as planned.
