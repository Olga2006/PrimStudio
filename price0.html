There is no such thing as a «typical design».
Each one is so unique and different.
That is why it is very difficult to put flat fees on projects. For different steps of our job we have different methods of counting of fee. We divided all project for 8 steps, so you can choose what you wish to give us and what you would like to realize yourself.



OUR SERVICES



DECORATION ADVICES


► Advices for arrangement of your interior

► Assistance in a choice of the furniture and decoration

► Assistance in a choice of finishing’s materials

Price*: 80€(excluding VAT)/hour, 250€(excluding VAT)/half day, 400€(excluding VAT)/day

(The tariff is calculated in the radius of 50 km from Mandelieu-la-Napoule, beyond: 50€ is charged per hour of driving)


INTERIOR DESIGN PROJECT


1. PROJECT BRIEFING


2. RESEARCH OF STATE OF BUILDING

► Identification of the initial state of the object

► Measurements of the object

► Plan 2D


3. DEFINITIVE PROJECT

► Plan-view

► 3D visualization

► Construction documentation (plan of alteration / extensions, lighting, plans of finishing of floors / walls / ceilings, joinery, ...)

► Specification of materials: the preliminary selection of the materials and equipments (materials of finishing of floors / walls / ceilings, sanitary, furniture)

► 3D modeling and drawings of a bespoke furniture

Price*: From €/m² (excluding VAT)

(The price is calculated on a basis of the relevant area of the rooms, depending on your preferred style, type of the premises and scope of the project) The minimum fee is 500€ (excluding VAT)


PROJECT MANAGEMENT


► Consultation of executing firms for preparation of specifications

► Assistance in the final choice of the materials

► Monitoring of the finishing of floors, walls and ceiling (painter, plasterer, tiler, carpenter, carpenter, ...)

► Manage of orders and deliveries (equipment, furniture, textiles, decorations)

► Controlling of an installation of the furniture and decorative elements (woodworkers, suppliers)

Price*: 5-10% of the amount excluding VAT of the orders



HOME STAGING


► Plan-view 2D your property - the general plan

► 3D visualization of each room, including: furnitures, textiles, decorations, lighting, floor's, wall's and ceiling's coverings

Price*: From 15€/m² (excluding VAT)



LANDSCAPE DESIGN PROJECT


► 2D schematic plan of the landscape (2 versions)

► 3D visualization

► Construction drawings (Dimension's plans, plans of covering, lighting, planting and installation of an outdoor living elements - outdoor kitchens, fire pits, etc.)

► Specification of materials: the preliminary selection of materials and equipments (coverings, plants, furniture and decorative items for the garden)

► 3D modeling and drawings of a bespoke furniture

Price*: From 2200€/100m² (excluding VAT)

(The price is calculated on a basis of the relevant area, depending on your preferred style and scope of the project) The minimum fee is 500€ (excluding VAT)
